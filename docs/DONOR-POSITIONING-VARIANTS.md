# Donor-Specific Positioning Statements

## USAID (Democracy, Rights, Governance)

**Origin Statement:**
IVYAR is a U.S.-developed governance platform aligned with USAID's priorities in transparency, anti-corruption, and democratic governance. Built on U.S. infrastructure and compliant with NIST and federal cybersecurity standards, IVYAR strengthens accountability and supports partner countries in advancing open, citizen-centered governance.

**Use in:** USAID proposals, DRG programs, anti-corruption initiatives

---

## NATO DIANA (Defense Innovation)

**Origin Statement:**
IVYAR was engineered in the United States with a security-first architecture aligned to NATO cyber and resilience principles. The platform integrates Zero-Trust, AI-driven anomaly detection, and multi-tenant isolation, making it suitable for dual-use civilian and defense governance environments.

**Use in:** NATO DIANA applications, defense ministry briefings, dual-use technology programs

---

## World Bank (Digital Development)

**Origin Statement:**
IVYAR is a U.S.-origin digital governance platform designed to strengthen institutional capacity, improve transparency, and support large-scale public-sector modernization. Developed within the U.S. regulatory and cybersecurity ecosystem, IVYAR aligns with World Bank digital governance frameworks and public financial management standards.

**Use in:** World Bank project proposals, institutional capacity building, digital transformation programs

---

## European Commission (Digital Europe)

**Origin Statement:**
IVYAR combines transatlantic partnership values with cutting-edge technology. Developed in the United States and aligned with EU digital governance principles, IVYAR supports European institutions in achieving transparency, cybersecurity, and digital sovereignty goals while maintaining interoperability with U.S. and NATO systems.

**Use in:** EU funding applications, Digital Europe Programme proposals, transatlantic cooperation initiatives

---

## Government of Canada (Digital Government)

**Origin Statement:**
IVYAR is a North American governance technology initiative developed in the United States with alignment to Canadian digital government standards. The platform supports Canada's commitment to open government, digital service delivery, and multi-level governance coordination.

**Use in:** Canadian government procurement, provincial partnerships, North American cooperation programs

---

## Ukrainian Government & Reconstruction

**Origin Statement (Ukrainian):**
IVYAR створений українцем, який живе і працює у США. Платформа розроблена на американських стандартах безпеки та прозорості, але з українським баченням, досвідом і цінностями. Це спільний внесок України та США у майбутнє прозорого державного управління.

**Origin Statement (English):**
IVYAR was created by a Ukrainian living and working in the United States. The platform is built on American security and transparency standards, but with Ukrainian vision, experience, and values. This is a joint contribution from Ukraine and the United States to the future of transparent governance.

**Use in:** Ukrainian government presentations, reconstruction programs, bilateral cooperation initiatives
