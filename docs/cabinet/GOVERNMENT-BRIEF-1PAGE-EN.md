# AUTOPILOT DECISION ENGINE
## One-Page Government Brief

**For:** Cabinet of Ministers, Presidential Office, International Partners  
**Date:** January 2026  
**Status:** Production-Ready

---

### THE PROBLEM
Different regions make different decisions under identical conditions. Lack of unified criteria, difficult audits, high operator workload, corruption and subjectivity risks.

### THE SOLUTION
**Autopilot Decision Engine** — a national decision support system that analyzes cases using predefined scenarios, government regulations, and controlled reasoning.

### HOW IT WORKS
1. Operator selects scenario
2. Inputs case data
3. System analyzes using regulations
4. Returns structured decision with explanation
5. Logs everything for audit

### PRODUCTION SCENARIOS
✅ Construction Risk Assessment  
✅ Procurement Integrity Check  
✅ Social Support Case Evaluation

### BENEFITS FOR THE STATE
- Unified decision standards across regions
- Transparent and auditable processes
- Reduced personnel workload
- Lower corruption risks
- Increased citizen trust

### BENEFITS FOR INTERNATIONAL PARTNERS
- Full audit trail
- Reproducible decisions
- Donor-grade transparency
- Independent verification
