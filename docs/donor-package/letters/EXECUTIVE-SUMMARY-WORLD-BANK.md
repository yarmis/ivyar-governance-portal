# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY — WORLD BANK FORMAT
**Autopilot Decision Engine**

---

## Overview

The **Autopilot Decision Engine** is an operational digital module designed to strengthen institutional governance capacity, improve decision consistency, and reduce fiduciary and operational risks across public administration processes in Ukraine.

The system provides scenario-based, rule-driven, and fully auditable decision support aligned with international governance and accountability standards.

Autopilot enhances transparency, strengthens internal controls, and supports long-term public sector modernization efforts.

---

## Governance Challenge

Public administration processes in Ukraine face structural constraints:

- Inconsistent interpretation and application of rules across regions
- Limited transparency and auditability of administrative decisions
- High administrative burden on frontline staff
- Elevated fiduciary and operational risks
- Fragmented monitoring and oversight mechanisms

These challenges hinder institutional performance and reduce the effectiveness of public service delivery.

---

## Solution Summary

Autopilot provides:

- **Standardized decision logic** across administrative units
- **Explainable outcomes** with references to rules and criteria
- **Immutable audit logs** for verification and oversight
- **Reproducible decisions**, reducing discretion and variability
- **Integration with existing workflows**, supporting institutional adoption

The system is currently operational with **three production scenarios**:

1. **Construction Risk Assessment** — Supports infrastructure governance and risk mitigation
2. **Procurement Integrity Check** — Strengthens procurement oversight and compliance
3. **Social Support Case Evaluation** — Improves equity and consistency in social protection programs
