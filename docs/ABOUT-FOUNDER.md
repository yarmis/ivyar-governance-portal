## U.S. Origin & Leadership

IVYAR was founded by **Igor Yarmosyuk**, a Ukrainian-born architect living in the United States. The platform was designed, engineered, and secured in the U.S., reflecting American leadership in digital governance, cybersecurity, and ethical AI.

We acknowledge with gratitude the opportunities, standards, and innovation ecosystem provided by the United States, which enabled IVYAR to reach global, government-grade quality.

**Built in the United States. Shaped by Ukrainian resilience. Designed for governments worldwide.**

---

## Founder Background

**Igor Yarmosyuk** specializes in secure, scalable, AI-powered government systems. His work focuses on creating infrastructure that strengthens trust, accountability, and operational integrity across governments, donors, and international partners.

With a background in designing multi-country governance systems, Igor combines Ukrainian resilience with U.S. institutional rigor to build ethical, transparent public-sector infrastructure.
