## ğŸ“˜ 5. Ethical Stop Signals (ESS)

A mechanism that blocks any action violating ethical norms.

| Signal | Trigger | Response |
|--------|---------|----------|
| **ESS-1: Harm Risk Detected** | Potential harm identified | Block action, escalate to human |
| **ESS-2: Bias Detected** | Any indication of bias | Block action, trigger review |
| **ESS-3: Insufficient Explainability** | System cannot explain decision | Block action, require clarification |
| **ESS-4: Compliance Violation** | GDPR, ISO, UN, OECD breach | Block action, alert compliance team |
| **ESS-5: Human Override Required** | High-stakes decision | Halt and await human authorization |
