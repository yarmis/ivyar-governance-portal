## 4. SLA Framework

### Standard Rules

| Event | Deadline | Tolerance |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| Initial Response | 3 business days | 1 day |
| Document Review | 10 business days | 2 days |
| Claim Decision | 14 business days | 3 days |
| Payment Processing | 14 days | 3 days |
| Appeal Response | 30 days | 5 days |

### Severity Classification

| Level | Days Overdue | Action |
|-------|--------------|--------|
| Minor | 1-4 days | Warning |
| Major | 5-9 days | Escalation |
| Critical | 10+ days | Legal flag |
